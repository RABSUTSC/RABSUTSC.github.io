Welcome to the village

The plot of this game revolves around a classic adventure scenario, where a young warrior named Janie arrives in a troubled town. The villagers are threatened by a beast that destroys their crops at night.

In this video game, you take on the role of the player and have the option to accept or reject the mission. If you choose to accept it, Janie embarks on a quest to confront the monster. After the battle, Janie returns to the town, where she receives a gold chest as a reward for completing the mission.

Janie's Adventures - The Village

Janie's Arrival

Game Date: Medieval Age

The plot of this game is based on a classic adventure scenario, in which a young warrior named Janie arrives in a city in trouble. The villagers are threatened by a beast that destroys their crops at night. The village chief assigns Yani the task of defeating this monster, since the villagers are farmers and cannot cope with the threat of the terrible beast.

Main characters

Meet Juany and the Village Leader

Juany: A brave 23-year-old young man, with a magic sword, resistance and skills in communicating with forest creatures. Join him in his quest to defeat the monster!
Village Leader: A skilled strategist with a connection to nature. His inspiring bravery guides Juany and the villagers in the fight against dark forces.

The Beast of Darkness

Get ready to face the monster!

The Monster of Darkness: A fearsome creature with dark magical abilities, transformation and regeneration. Can you defeat him and save the village?
